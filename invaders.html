Alien Invaders

This was the final project for my CS 1110: Intro to Python course in Fall 2018. It works similarly to the version you'd find anywhere on the internet.

Your screen starts with this when you first run the game:


Once 'S' has been pressed, the game begins. As you see in the picture below, you have to shoot at the aliens, but the aliens will be moving across the screen and shooting at you.


You will move your spaceship left and right to try to destroy the aliens and avoid their shots. If you get shot, you lose a life and the pause screen appears.


Your score is kept track of in the top left-hand corner (destroying closer aliens gets you less points than destroying aliens farther away), and the number of lives you have left is displayed in the top right-hand corner. You lose the game if you lose all your lives...


or if the aliens reach the line on the bottom before you destroy all of them.


The video below shows a playthrough of the game. The game has 4 waves, and in each wave the aliens move 25% faster across the screen. The number of lives reset for each wave so the game isn't too difficult. I unfortunately couldn't beat my own game, but it can be done!
