This was the final project for my CS 2110: Object-Oriented Programming and Data Structures course in Spring 2019. A new maze is presented every time, and there are two stages. In the first stage, the character tries to find the ring by visiting every possible path. Then, the character tries to collect the most number of coins and get to the exit before running out of steps.
